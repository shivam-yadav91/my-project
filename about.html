Our Story

Founded in 2010, LuxeHotel started with a simple vision: to create a place where modern luxury and timeless elegance meet. Our founders traveled the world, gathering inspiration to build a hotel that is not just a place to stay, but an experience to remember.


LuxeHotels legacy began nearly four decades ago with a dream to craft unforgettable stays. What started as a humble boutique hotel is now a landmark of refined luxury and personalized experiences.
